Symphonic pieces

The First Vision

This is a piece that I wrote, attempting to tell a story through only music. It's a love story, and I was pretty happy with how it came out. Two themes, represented most often by specific instruments, fall in love, grow together, grow apart, and ultimately reconcile. The themes weave together and perform calls-and-responses as they interact to tell the story.

The idea itself is I guess, sort of inspired by, or at least derived from Peter and the Wolf. Holtz described his suite, The Planets, as a "series of mood pictures." I think I like that description the best, but pieces like this are also referred to as program music or programmatic music, as well as symphonic poems.
